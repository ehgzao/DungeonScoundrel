1. Introduction

Welcome to Dungeon Scoundrel ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our game and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Email Address: When you sign up for our mobile waitlist or report bugs
  • Bug Reports: Technical information you provide when reporting issues
  • Consent Records: Your acceptance of privacy and marketing communications

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Browser type, version, operating system
  • Screen Resolution: Display size and pixel ratio
  • Game Progress: Saved locally in your browser (localStorage)
  • Cloud Saves: If you choose to sync with Google account

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Mobile Waitlist: To notify you when the mobile version launches
  • Bug Reports: To improve game quality and fix technical issues
  • Marketing Communications: To send tips, news, and updates (only with your consent)
  • Game Functionality: To save your progress and provide cloud sync

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR/LGPD)

We process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: You have explicitly agreed to receive communications
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our game and fix bugs
  • Contract Performance: To provide cloud save functionality

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Local Storage: Game progress stored in your browser
  • Cloud Storage: Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) for cloud saves
  • Email Service: EmailJS for communications
  • Encryption: All data transmitted over HTTPS

6. Your Rights (GDPR/LGPD)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Unsubscribe from communications at any time

7. Data Retention

  • Waitlist Emails: Retained until mobile version launches or you request deletion
  • Bug Reports: Retained for 2 years for quality improvement
  • Game Progress: Stored indefinitely in your browser or cloud account

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Cloud save functionality (Privacy Policy)
  • EmailJS: Email communications (Privacy Policy)
  • Netlify: Website hosting (Privacy Policy)

9. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to save your game progress locally. This is not a cookie and does not track you across websites. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

10. Children's Privacy

Our game is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with GDPR and LGPD requirements.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us:
